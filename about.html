About Us

Founded in 2006, co-owners Kazuma Kiryu and Gex the Gecko have been serving the community of Kamurocho for nearly 20 years.
Despite its rough start, the restaraunt has become a hotspot for locals; not just for food, but for community.
Kiryugex's has hosted several short-term events and has also had stands at local festivals.

Feel free to stop by and dine like a dragon. All are welcome.
